  1. Appointed as Assistant

    Time for a break, Doctor. Just how long have you been sitting at a table like that? Get up and go stretch out a little. Yes, I mean now.

  2. Talk 1

    It is my duty to perform regular check-ups on Oripathy patients. Some are personable enough, but a few are hard to communicate with. It's not the first time, though, so worry not, they will understand how vital a doctor is to them.

  3. Talk 2

    You know what an amnesia patient should pay attention to, Doctor? Don't work yourself into the ground. Work and relaxation are equally important!

  4. Talk 3

    You always have patients who ignore the doctor's orders, but in battle, you don't see a doctor who'd miss a single cry for help.

  5. Talk after Promotion 1

    The advancement of medicine is a lesson taught by none other than the cruelty of war and disasters, a lesson we medical workers learn through pain and suffering... But... It's a fact I figured out long ago. As long as I can help my patients get their lives back, I'm ready to face the fact.

  6. Talk after Promotion 2

    Together we've spent some... uh, I mean, some most memorable moments! Come on, you know I'm not good at this, Doctor. And next time I see you put up that teasing face, I'd be real mad!

  7. Talk after Trust Increase 1

    Here's a pen. How did I know you wanted it? Well, I've been working with you for a while now, isn't it natural I picked up on it?

  8. Talk after Trust Increase 2

    I'm still far below the level of Silence and Shining, but that doesn't mean I can't overcome an obstacle when I see one before me. Otherwise, how can I measure up to your confidence in me?

  9. Talk after Trust Increase 3

    The sun's good on the deck, and the wind's just fine… Mm? No, I'm not slacking off. It's just... I'm feeling swamped right now, like you occasionally do, Doctor. So, anything bothering you? Mm-hm, keep it as a whisper if it's easier for you this way. I'm listening.
